ER Diagram (Text Version)

USERS
-----
user_id (PK)
user_name
email

MOVIES
-----
movie_id (PK)
title
genre
release_year

RATINGS
-------
rating_id (PK)
user_id (FK → USERS.user_id)
movie_id (FK → MOVIES.movie_id)
rating (1–5)
rating_date

Relationships:
- One USER → Many RATINGS
- One MOVIE → Many RATINGS
